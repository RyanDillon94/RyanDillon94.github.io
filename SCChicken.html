Slow Cooker Chicken

  • Description
  • Ingredients
  • Method
  • Nutrition

Description

Easy, full of flavour and hella versatile!
This Slow Cooker Chicken prep is as easy as it comes... throw it all in the Slow Cooker and let it cook before shredding it and enjoying it however you like throughout the week.


Ingredients

  • 1 Red Onion
  • 1kg Chicken Breast
  • Seasoning of your choice(2 packets,80g)


Method

  1. Spray the Slow Cooker with 1kcal spray
  2. Slice the Red Onion thinly and add to the Slow Cooker
  3. Trim and add the Chicken Breasts to the Slow Cooker
  4. Add the Seasoning to the Slow Cooker
  5. Mix well
  6. Cook on Low for 6 hours or High for 4 hours
  7. Shred the Chicken and mix in the juices
  8. Enjoy!
