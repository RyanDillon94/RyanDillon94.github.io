Savoury Rice

  • Description
  • Ingredients
  • Method
  • Nutrition

Description

Savoury rice is a hearty rice based dish. It has a curry flavour and is full of vegetables, making it the perfect low calorie meal prep dish.



Ingredients

  • 500g Peppers
  • 1 White Onion
  • 300g Carrots
  • 250g Sweetcorn
  • 2 Vegetable Stock cubes
  • 20g Curry Powder
  • 300g Basmati Rice/Orzo


Method

  1. Dice the Onion and fry in 1kcal spray
  2. While the Onions soften, dice the Peppers and Carrots
  3. Once the Onion has softened, add the Peppers and Carrots to the pan
  4. Disolve the Vegetable Stock in 1 litre of boiling water
  5. Add the Sweetcorn to the pan
  6. Add the Rice/Orzo to the pan
  7. Add the Curry Powder to the pan
  8. Mix well
  9. Add the Stock and mix thoroughly
  10. Bring to the boil over high heat
  11. Reduce the heat and simmer for approximately 13-15 minutes
  12. Enjoy!
